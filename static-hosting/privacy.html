üìã Overview

This privacy policy explains how the Scheduled Website Opener Chrome extension handles your data. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring complete transparency about our data practices.

üîç What Data We Collect

We collect absolutely no personal information. The extension operates entirely locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage APIs.

Local Data Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • Website URLs: The websites you schedule to open
  • Schedule Times: The times you set for automatic opening
  • Recurrence Settings: Whether schedules are daily, weekly, monthly, etc.
  • Extension Preferences: Your personal settings and configurations

üîê How Your Data is Handled

All your schedule data stays entirely on your computer. We do not send any data to external servers, third parties, or our own servers. Your data is processed using Chrome's local storage APIs:

  • chrome.storage.local - Stores your schedule data locally
  • chrome.alarms - Manages scheduled triggers
  • chrome.tabs - Opens websites when scheduled
