Privacy Policy for Scheduled Website Opener

This extension helps users schedule websites to open at specific times (daily, weekly, monthly, etc.).

What Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. All data related to your schedule is stored locally in your browser using the Chrome storage API.

Permissions Used

  • Storage: To store the schedule list.
  • Alarms: To trigger the website open actions.
  • Tabs: To open scheduled websites in new browser tabs.

How Your Data is Handled

Your schedule data stays entirely on your computer. We do not send any data to external servers.
