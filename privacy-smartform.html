SmartForm Architect Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2026-02-28

🛡️ Privacy First

We have built SmartForm Architect from the ground up to respect and protect your data. Your privacy is critically important to us.

1. Introduction

Welcome to SmartForm Architect ("we," "our," "us"). This policy explains how we handle your information and our commitment to your privacy.

2. Information We Collect

SmartForm Architect operates entirely locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal information on any external servers.

  • Form Data & Templates: Any form structures, fields, and default values you record or save using the extension are stored strictly locally on your browser local storage using the chrome.storage.local API.
  • Settings: Your UI preferences and functional toggles are saved locally.

3. How We Use the Information

The locally saved information is used solely for the purpose of the extension's functional features:

  • To intelligently suggest autofill values based on your saved templates.
  • To execute the global form autofill when triggered via the keyboard shortcut or per-page banner.

4. Third-Party Access

Because all your data remains locally on your device, we do not share, sell, or rent it to any third parties.

5. Security

While your data never leaves your device from our extension, you are responsible for the physical and digital security of the device where Chrome is installed. We utilize Chrome's secure storage API for all extension configuration and data saving needs.

6. Your Rights

You have absolute control over your data:

  • You can edit or delete any stored templates directly from the SmartForm Architect Dashboard.
  • You can instantly wipe all stored data by utilizing Chrome's extension management to clear the extension's local data or by uninstalling the extension entirely.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy periodically. Since we collect no email addresses to notify you, we encourage you to review this policy whenever a new major update of the extension drops.
